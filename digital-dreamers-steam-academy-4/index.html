About Us

Do you want to learn how to code, create games, design websites, and more? If you are a student in grades 6-12 and live in San Jose, you can join the Digital Dreamers Academy, an after school computer science learning program that will help you develop your skills and passion for technology. At Digital Dreamers Academy, you will work on fun and engaging projects with the guidance of experienced instructors and mentors. You will also have the opportunity to showcase your work, connect with other students, and explore career paths in the tech industry. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced coder, Digital Dreamers Academy has something for you. Enroll today and become a digital dreamer!
